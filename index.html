Biography

    I am a Machine Learning researcher with research interests in statistical machine learning methods, and applications to healthcare and robotics. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Machine Learning Department @ MBZUAI, working with Prof. Bin Gu and Prof. Huan Xiong, with a focus on energy-efficient AI and distributed computing, especially on Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs). If you are a PhD student or postdoc interested in working with me, please email me.

    Before joining MBZUAI, from July 2020 to June 2022, I was a Senior Research Scientist in the Big Data Lab of Baidu Research (Beijing), Baidu Inc.. During this period, I worked with Dr. Haoyi Xiong and Prof. Dejing Dou on the project focused on Model Selection using Machine Learning Methods and Optimization. I was a visiting researcher in Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics @ HKUST from 2018 to 2019, hosted by Prof. Fugee TSUNG on the project of Outlier Detection in Mobile Phones.

    I did my Ph.D. in Statistics at School of Statistics and Data Science of Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Changliang Zou and Prof. Zhaojun Wang. My PhD focused on change-point detection of time-series data. I did my M.S. in Probability and Statistics at Lanzhou University, supervised by Prof. Jianzhou Wang, and I did my B.S. in Mathematics at Lanzhou University.
